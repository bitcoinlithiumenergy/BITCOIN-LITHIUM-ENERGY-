# Bitcoin Lithium Energy (BLE) ⚡

**BLE = Bitcoin Lithium Energy**, a Solana-based cryptocurrency project focused on green energy, sustainable solutions, and crypto-enabled payments.  
⚠️ Note: BLE here is **NOT related to Bluetooth Low Energy**.

---

## Feature Project Vision

Bitcoin Lithium Energy aims to create a future-ready, blockchain-powered energy ecosystem:

- **Affordable Home Energy:** Provide low-cost energy supply for households.  
- **EV Charging Infrastructure:** Own and operate electric vehicle charging stations globally.  
- **Crypto Payment Integration:** Accept cryptocurrency for energy and services to drive a decentralized economy.  
- **Green Energy Development:** Build sustainable energy projects, focusing on renewable sources.  
- **Global Utility Token:** Enable BLE as a Solana-based utility token for real-world transactions, investments, and governance.  
- **Future Innovation:** Expand BLE ecosystem with multiple energy, technology, and crypto-based solutions under our company’s development vision.
